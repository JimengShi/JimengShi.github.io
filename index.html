Introduction

Hi there! I am a postdoctoral research associate at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), working with Prof. Shaowen Wang (advisor) and Prof. Jiawei Han. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from Florida International University (FIU), advised by Prof. Giri Narasimhan. My research focus on developing and applying AI tools to address real-world problems in Natural Science and Earth Science. The AI for flood prediction and management work has been featured by FIU News and selected as The Year in Science: The most impactful research stories of 2025, and featured by South Florida Business Journal. This work has also attracted attenion from Florida House Natural Resources & Disasters Subcommittee, and has been presented to the hearing [video]. Sincere gratitude to NSF I-GUIDE, a research center for consistently supporting my work.

[Call for collaboration] I am working on a project on document compression (e.g., DeepSeek-OCR) and agents with reinforcement learning. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in it.

Research Interests

  • AI for Science and Engineering
    • Environment: flood prediction (WaLeF, FloodGTN, ReFine), flood mitigation (FIDLAr), physics-informed neural networks (PINNs), explainable AI with information bottleneck (TimeX++).
    • Climate: diffusion model for weather prediction (CoDiCast), foundation models for weather (Survey)
    • Biology: vision transformer for protein binding (PIsToN), evaluating protein docking models (Survey)
  • Data Mining to Boost LLMs
    • Retrieval and Structuring: Structured RAG (HyperCube-RAG, MultiCube-RAG)
    • In-domain Foundation Models: Benchmark (Water-Bench), Retrieval-Augmention Forecast (Water-RAF)
  • Agentic AI & RL
    • Tool Calling: coming soon
    • Multi-Agent with RL: coming soon
