Introduction

Hi there! I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), working with Prof. Shaowen Wang (advisor) and Prof. Jiawei Han. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from Florida International University (FIU), advised by Prof. Giri Narasimhan. My research focus on developing and applying AI tools to address real-world problems in Natural Science and Earth Science, while FIU has featured my work multiple times [link1, link2, link3]. South Florida Business Journal also featured my AI for flood prediction and control project. Recently, I have been working on LLMs, synergistic agents, and reinforcement learning (RL). Thank NSF I-GUIDE, a research consortium, for consistently supporting my work.

[Call for collaboration] I am working on a project on document compression (e.g., DeepSeek-OCR) and agents with reinforcement learning. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in it.

Research Interests

  • AI for Science
    • Environment: flood prediction (FloodGTN) and mitigation (FIDLAr), air quality prediction (AIR), physics-informed neural networks (PINNs)
    • Climate: diffusion model for weather prediction (CoDiCast), RAG for precipitation nowcast (coming soon)
    • Biology: vision transformer for protein binding (PIsToN), evaluating protein docking models (Survey)
  • Large Models
    • Large Language Models: RAG for LLMs (Hypercube-RAG)
    • Time Series Foundation Models: Benchmark (Water-Bench), Retrieval-Augmention Forecast (Water-RAF)
  • Explainable AI
    • Time Series: explanations with information bottleneck (TimeX++), explanations using LIME (ReFine, FIDLAr), explainations using GradCAM (ParaRCNN)
