Introduction

My name is Jimeng Shi, a CS Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Giri Narasimhan from Knight Foundation School of Computing and Information Sciences at Florida International University. My reasearch focus is to develop and apply algorithms of Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning to address versatile problems in the real world.
